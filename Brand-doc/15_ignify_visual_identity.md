# Ignify -- Visual Identity

## Personality

Bold, energetic, forward-moving, data-driven.

## Color Directions

Primary: Electric Orange or Gradient Red/Orange (spark, ignite).
Secondary: Deep navy / charcoal. Accent: neon or light gradient for tech
feeling.

## Typography

Modern geometric sans-serif.

## Logo Ideas

-   Spark turning into growth arrow.
-   Flame transforming into graph.
-   Minimal wordmark with dynamic cut.

## UI Feel

Dark + vibrant highlights.
