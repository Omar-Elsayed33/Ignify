# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Vision

Build an AI growth engine that automates SEO, content, ads, and lead
management.

## Target Market

SMEs, franchises, healthcare, education, restaurants, B2B.

## Value Proposition

Lower cost, faster execution, measurable growth.

## Principles

Approval first. AI second. Full transparency.

## Modules

-   Tenant Management
-   Identity & Access
-   Integrations
-   Ingestion
-   SEO Intelligence
-   Competitor Intelligence
-   Content Engine
-   Creative Engine
-   Ads Orchestration
-   Workflow
-   Lead CRM
-   Reporting

## Security

Separate DB per tenant, centralized token vault.
